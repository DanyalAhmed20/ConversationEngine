App description

Sign language is a non-verbal means of communication that is globally recognized and is used by the deaf and dumb people to communicate with others. It allows people to express themselves and understand each other without speaking. The purpose of building a conversation engine for the deaf and dumb is overcome the limitations they experience daily and to enhance their independence. The proposed model uses a convolution neural network that is trained on different hand gestures by abstracting the important features. It is trained using the American Sign Language dataset. It takes in different hand gestures and converts them into text and also takes in the speech from normal users and converts them into the text to be understood by the deaf and dumb people to improve the interaction between them.
